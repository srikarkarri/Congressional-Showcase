The Problem

Cancer is Complex.
Treatment Decisions Shouldn't Be.

Every cancer patient has a unique genomic fingerprint. Yet oncologists face an overwhelming volume of data, literature, and treatment options when making critical life-or-death decisions.

🧬

Genomic Data Overload

Modern tumor sequencing panels identify hundreds of mutations. Manually interpreting each variant's clinical significance takes days of expert analysis.

⏳

Delayed Treatment Decisions

Molecular Tumor Boards meet infrequently, causing weeks of delay between diagnosis and targeted therapy initiation — time patients can't afford to lose.

📚

Knowledge Gap

The biomedical literature grows by thousands of papers daily. No single oncologist can stay current on every mutation, drug interaction, and emerging trial.

What if AI could read every paper, know every mutation,
and surface the right answer — instantly?

How It Works

From Genome to Treatment Plan

OncoGAT transforms raw genomic sequencing data into actionable clinical insights through a streamlined AI pipeline.

01

Enter Patient Case Details

Clinicians input the patient's diagnosis, cancer type, stage, prior treatment history, and biomarker status. OncoGAT builds a structured clinical profile ready for AI-powered analysis.

🏥 Structured Patient Intake
02

Submit Genomic Mutations

Enter key somatic mutations (e.g. BRAF V600E, KRAS G12C, TP53 R175H) or paste NGS panel results. OncoGAT cross-references each variant against OncoKB, ClinVar, and COSMIC to assign clinical tier and actionability.

🧬 Variant Interpretation Engine
03

AI MTB Analysis & Recommendations

The AI co-pilot synthesizes all inputs and generates a ranked list of targeted therapies (FDA-approved and off-label), resistance mechanisms, and matched open clinical trials — with full evidence citations.

🤖 AI-Powered Treatment Planning
04

Interactive Q&A with the AI Co-Pilot

During the MTB meeting, oncologists ask plain-English follow-ups: "What resistance mechanisms are expected with vemurafenib?" or "Are there trials for co-mutated BRAF + PIK3CA?" — answered with cited evidence in real time.

💬 Live MTB Decision Support
05

Export Structured MTB Report

Generate a clean, comprehensive Molecular Tumor Board report summarizing the case, genomic findings, AI recommendations, and clinical trial matches — ready for documentation and multidisciplinary review.

📋 One-Click Report Generation
Key Features

Everything Your
MTB Needs

OncoGAT combines cutting-edge AI with deep oncology expertise to give tumor boards a decisive advantage.

🏥

MTB Case Dashboard

A dedicated Molecular Tumor Board workspace to manage patient cases — input diagnoses, cancer stage, biomarkers, and treatment history in a clean, structured interface built for clinical workflows.

🧬

Somatic Mutation Interpreter

Enter any mutation (BRAF V600E, KRAS G12C, HER2 amplification…) and get instant clinical tier classification, pathogenicity assessment, and functional impact — powered by OncoKB and COSMIC databases.

🎯

Targeted Therapy Recommender

AI ranks FDA-approved targeted therapies, immunotherapy options, and off-label treatments by evidence level (1A–4), expected response rate, and known resistance profiles for the patient's exact mutation set.

🔬

Clinical Trial Matcher

Automatically surfaces open ClinicalTrials.gov studies matching the patient's tumor type, genomic alterations, and eligibility criteria — with direct enrollment contact links.

🤖

AI MTB Co-Pilot

Ask anything mid-meeting: drug interactions, co-mutation resistance patterns, NCCN guideline references, or literature summaries. The AI co-pilot answers with cited, evidence-graded responses in seconds.

📋

One-Click MTB Report

Export a fully structured Molecular Tumor Board report — case summary, variant analysis, therapy recommendations, trial matches, and AI rationale — ready for clinical documentation and EHR upload.
